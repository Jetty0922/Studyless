Vocabulary List - Chapter 5

1. Ephemeral - lasting for a very short time
2. Ubiquitous - present, appearing, or found everywhere
3. Pragmatic - dealing with things sensibly and realistically
4. Ameliorate - to make something bad or unsatisfactory better
5. Enigmatic - difficult to interpret or understand; mysterious
6. Verbose - using or expressed in more words than are needed
7. Meticulous - showing great attention to detail; very careful
8. Lucid - expressed clearly; easy to understand
9. Tenacious - not readily letting go of, giving up, or separated from
10. Benevolent - well meaning and kindly
