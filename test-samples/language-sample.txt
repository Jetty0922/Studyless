Spanish - Common Phrases

¿Cómo estás? - How are you? (informal)
Use with friends, family, and people your age.

Me gustaría reservar una mesa - I would like to reserve a table
Formal phrase used in restaurants.

¿Dónde está el baño? - Where is the bathroom?
Essential phrase for travelers.

No entiendo - I don't understand
Useful when learning or in conversation.

¿Cuánto cuesta? - How much does it cost?
Important for shopping and transactions.

Con permiso - Excuse me (when passing by)
Polite phrase to get through a crowd.

¡Qué aproveche! - Enjoy your meal!
Said before eating, similar to "bon appétit"

Disculpe, ¿puede ayudarme? - Excuse me, can you help me?
Formal way to ask for assistance.
